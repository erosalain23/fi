School Connect and Control System (XulCo) will be a cross-platform, web-based application that will allow a particular school to connect all aspects that are needed on a daily basis together, starting from academics, discipline and social life up to finances of students at school. Ultimately, by mustering altogether, the application will provide them with a way of controlling every facet to benefits students and schools. This system will allow them to use their time effectively, access the useful information easily, and learn to the optimal of their potential with the XulCo’s ground of sharing academic materials and intellectual ideas, as well as allowing parents to do follow-ups on their children while they are at school. To begin with, XulCo will incorporate an upgraded version of our former library management system. In the library system, students are able to borrow books quickly without waiting on queues and serve themselves, which leaves for a librarian a task of ensuring the book taken is the right one. This system also consists of some reporting and analysis programs that help a school to make some statistics and students who still owe books at the end of academic terms, among other tons of useful information it can provide. In XulCo, we are planning to upgrade this system with barcode scanning method to speed up the process of lending books. Also, we plan to make a cloud storage of soft books, where any one will share some soft books so that other will access them, and the program will recommend some books in respect of each individual’s interests and combinations of studies. Moreover, recommendations from word wide libraries to students will be offered by the App. In addition, the financial data will be recorded in the system to help bursars, students, and schools to use and access information they are privilege to obtain, in lieu of rolling out piles of documents as they are ordinary kept. On our platform students will be opened to a new structure of sharing their ideas and ask questions, where teachers or other people from accredited academia with a particular knowledge on something will help answer those questions. Even parents will be connected to the system to an extent they will be able to know their children status in every part of those aspects. More importantly, this system will connect all schools that are using it and help them to collaborate to deliver adequate education to their students. And it will not be a static application, rather, it will be an evolving application that we will still develop to meet the needs of students, as later on, we will try to integrate some AI features as it get wider with data.
